Team Work Communication Support
Group 6 is committed to learning all that we can about working as a team throughout this semester. Sharing our efforts to create something greater than ourselves. No team can survive without good communication, and we're excited to put our skills to the test. We will work to build an environment of trust and empathy for the good of all. Everyone needs help some of the time, and an integral part of working as a team is sharing our strengths and weaknesses so that the group can overcome any challenge.
